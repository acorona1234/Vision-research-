## Key Findings

### ‚úÖ What Works (Supported by Multiple RCTs)
- **Outdoor time for kids** (2+ hrs/day) ‚Äî multiple studies show protective effect against myopia development
- **Low-dose atropine** (0.01-0.05%) ‚Äî studies suggest may slow myopia progression by roughly 50%, though results vary
- **AREDS2 formula** ‚Äî but ONLY for intermediate/advanced AMD
- **Screen breaks** ‚Äî 20-20-20 rule for digital eye strain
- **Preservative-free artificial tears** ‚Äî for dry eye/strain

### ‚ùå What Lacks Supporting Evidence (Despite Marketing)
- **Blue light blocking glasses** ‚Äî we found no high-quality RCT evidence for eye strain or protection benefits
- **Omega-3s for dry eye** ‚Äî DREAM trial showed no benefit vs placebo
- **Bilberry supplements** ‚Äî marketing exceeds evidence
- **Under-correcting kids' glasses** ‚Äî may actually worsen myopia
